Client

Personal Project

Project Description

This was a personal project. I wanted to create a simple and clean book that could potentially printed or simply viewed as an ebook.

My Contribution

I chose The Count of Monte Cristo because it is one of my favorite books. I wanted to make something that was not only modern and clean, but also classy. I chose a serif font that was versatile enough to be both the title and body fonts. It is big and bold as a title and clean and readable as a paragraph. I opted to keep the book colorless because of the dark and depressing content of the novel. I created this document in InDesign and modified the cover image in Photoshop.





Client

Office of Research and Graduate Studies at Utah State University

Project Description

This was a project for a folklore tour given to prospective students of USU. The constraints of the project were that it needed a quick turn around and needed to be able to be printed in house on an office copier. They also wanted to produce it at the lowest cost possible.

My Contribution

I opted to use a simple layout in black and white that would allow them to print on any printer without the need for a bleed. The final product was produced in InDesign and printed as a half page, saddle-stitched booklet. See the whole thing here: Booklet PDF





Client

Emily, The Yoga Tribe

Project Description

This is a yoga teacher training manual that I made for my client Emily. She uses this manual as an augmentation to her teaching as well as a reference for students. It includes spaces for students to take notes as they learn.

My Contribution

For this project, my client and I wanted to create something that reflected the essence of yoga. It needed to be functional as well as pretty, so we opted for something that was clean and calming. My client preferred purples and blues. Coincidentally, those colors have calming and peaceful properties that contribute to the overall yogic feel. I created the logo in Adobe Illustrator and laid out the manual in InDesign.
