Project Description

This animation was created for a series of videos that discussed 12 virtues of entrepreneurial ethics. The videos are a part of a Massive Open Online Course (MOOC).

My Contribution

The graphics for this video were built in Adobe Illustrator and then imported into After Effects for animation. The illustrations were intentionally kept simple and basic to let the viewer fill in the details. This ambiguity allows the video to fit more situations.
